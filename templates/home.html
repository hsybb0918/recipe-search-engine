Our Characteristics

Meet your different search needs for recipes

Regular Search

Search for the title, description and ingredients of the recipe, using BM25 and TFIDF scores to show the most relevant results.

Ingredient Search

Search by ingredients, then recommend recipes that contain specific ingredients to solve the problem of idle ingredients.

Time Filtering

Whether it is a light meal at work or a dinner party carefully prepared, time filtering gives what you want.

Autocomplete

Through the front-end and back-end asynchronous update technology, the key words automatically associates the recipe names.

Popularity Ranking

Allow to sort recipes according to the number of reviews or score of the recipe, saving the time in searching for your favorite recipes.

Recipes Recommendation

Combined with the most advanced machine learning algorithm KNN, we recommend the most similar recipes to the current one.
